About Kintsugi Holding

Kintsugi Holding is a leading, government-owned technology company based in the UAE, dedicated to developing solutions that address global problems and enhance human lives.

The company plays a pivotal role in enabling the UAE's strategic agenda to embrace technology and Artificial Intelligence, striving to position the nation at the forefront of global innovation.
